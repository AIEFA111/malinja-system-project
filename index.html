Welcome to Malinja Cafe

With support from the university administration and local partners, Malinja Cafe officially opened its doors inside UiTM Kedah, becoming a go-to destination for students, especially male students since itâ€™s closer to their dorm. Their vision is to bring students, lecturers, and visitors together through scrumptious food and welcoming service with a vibrant atmosphere. They strive to create a space where students can hang out and even hold group discussions while having delicious food. While their mission is to create excellent services and experience that brings out the energy of university life. This is done by serving fresh, nutritious, and delicious meals tailored to the tastes of the students. Moreover, Cafe Malinja also aims to create a comfortable and inviting environment where students can eat, socialize, and recharge with just a simple meal.
